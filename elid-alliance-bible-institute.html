Elid Alliance Bible Institute offers a 4-year Bachelor of Theology (Ecclesiastical) program designed to train and equip men and women for effective ministry in the church and mission field. The curriculum consists of 120 units, structured across 3 trimesters per year, with a strong focus on Church Planting, Urban Ministry, Pastoral Leadership, and Practical Ministry Development.

Each trimester includes three major subjects (3 units each) and one unit of Weekend Ministry, totaling 10 units per term. The final year is a supervised internship with emphasis on ministry reflection and church planting application based on personal experiences. The program is intentionally balanced between academic theology and real-world ministry skills.
